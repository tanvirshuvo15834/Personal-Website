A Little Bit About Myself

Hi there, I'm Tanvir Shuvo. I'm a Graphic designer. I started my career as a Graphics Designer on February 2016. Meanwhile I completed my Bachelors Degree in CSE from Daffodil International University, Bangladesh.

In between these times I gathered knowledge as well as improved my skill-sets. Now, I deliver designs for various soft materials for websites, mobile apps and in various digital platform. I also design for print media. I usually design from user personas, stories, use cases, Market research and analytics.

I have 3 years of experience in Graphics Designing. In between these time i worked with Different Organisation and companies like (Let's Furnish, Impact Summit Malaysia. I helped them with branding and art direction. Each venture brings my eye for detail and one of a kind capacity to mix structure and capacity. I'm glad to carry these abilities to Freelancing. I will be happy to find out about your undertaking and work with you.
